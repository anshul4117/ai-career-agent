# AI Career Agent — Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name

AI Career Agent

---

## Product Vision

AI Career Agent is an intelligent career platform that helps candidates discover high-quality job opportunities, optimize their application materials, and manage their complete job search journey.

The platform focuses on **quality over quantity**.

Instead of showing thousands of jobs, the platform identifies and recommends only the most relevant, trusted, active, and high-quality opportunities.

Our mission is to reduce job search time, eliminate spam opportunities, improve application quality, and increase interview conversion rates.

---

## Problem Statement

Current job platforms have several major issues:

### Job Discovery Problems

- Large number of duplicate job postings
- Outdated job listings
- Ghost jobs that are no longer actively hiring
- Low-quality and spam opportunities
- Irrelevant recommendations

### Application Problems

- Candidates repeatedly fill the same information
- Resume customization is time-consuming
- Cover letter writing requires significant effort
- Applications are difficult to track

### Career Management Problems

- No centralized job search workflow
- No personalized career guidance
- No clear visibility into application progress
- Lack of insights into skill gaps and job readiness

As a result, candidates spend excessive time searching and applying rather than preparing for interviews and improving their careers.

---

## Solution

AI Career Agent provides a centralized AI-powered career management system.

The platform will:

1. Create a structured candidate profile.
2. Search jobs across multiple trusted sources.
3. Filter duplicate, outdated, and low-quality jobs.
4. Match opportunities against candidate skills and goals.
5. Optimize resumes for specific job descriptions.
6. Generate personalized cover letters.
7. Track applications and hiring progress.
8. Provide career insights and recommendations.

---

## Core Features

### Candidate Profile

#### Resume Upload

Users can upload resumes.

The system extracts:

- Skills
- Experience
- Education
- Projects
- Certifications
- Contact Information

#### Career Preferences

Users can define:

- Preferred Role
- Preferred Location
- Employment Type
- Salary Expectations
- Work Preferences

---

### Job Discovery Engine

The platform collects jobs from:

- Company Career Pages
- Wellfound
- YC Jobs
- Internshala
- Trusted Job Sources

**Future integrations:**

- LinkedIn
- Naukri
- Indeed

---

### Job Quality Engine

Every job receives quality scores.

#### Freshness Score

Measures how recently the opportunity was posted.

#### Trust Score

Measures company credibility.

#### Relevance Score

Measures alignment with candidate profile.

#### Overall Quality Score

Final ranking score used for recommendations.

---

### AI Job Matching

The system compares:

**Candidate Profile** vs **Job Description**

**Outputs:**

- Match Percentage
- Matching Skills
- Missing Skills
- Strength Areas
- Improvement Suggestions

---

### Resume Optimization

AI customizes resumes for specific job descriptions.

**Features:**

- ATS optimization
- Keyword enhancement
- Skills highlighting
- Content improvement

---

### Cover Letter Generation

Generate personalized cover letters based on:

- Job Description
- Candidate Profile
- Company Information

---

### Application Tracker

**Application stages:**

- Saved
- Applied
- Interview Scheduled
- Interview Completed
- Rejected
- Offer Received
- Accepted

## Version 1 Scope

The following features will be included in Version 1:

- Authentication
- Candidate Profile
- Resume Upload
- Job Discovery
- Job Quality Engine
- AI Match Score
- Resume Optimization
- Cover Letter Generation
- Application Tracking

---

## Out of Scope (Version 1)

The following features will **not** be built initially:

- Automatic Job Applications
- Browser Automation
- Referral Discovery
- Interview Agent
- Salary Negotiation Agent
- AI Voice Assistant
- WhatsApp Automation
- Chrome Extension

These may be considered in future versions.

## Success Metrics

The platform will be considered successful when:

### User Metrics

- Users return weekly.
