# AI Career Agent — Brand Guidelines

Version: 1.0

Status: Active

Owner: Product & Design Team

---

# Brand Vision

AI Career Agent is not another job portal.

It is an AI-powered Career Operating System that helps people discover high-quality opportunities, optimize their careers, and get hired faster.

Our product represents intelligence, trust, simplicity, and quality.

---

# Brand Mission

Help every job seeker discover better opportunities with AI instead of endless searching.

---

# Brand Promise

Find High-Quality Jobs. Not Spam.

Every design decision should reinforce this promise.

## 3. Intelligence

Represented by

Connected Nodes

NOT the letters "AI"

The connected nodes symbolize

Machine Learning

Neural Networks

Career Matching

Recommendation Engine

Smart Decisions

Connected Data

Intelligence

Maintain consistent typography.
